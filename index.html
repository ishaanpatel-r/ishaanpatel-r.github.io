Transcending Into More

As we move towards an age of intelligent systems, we're faced with a dilemma - a rather thought-provoking one. Do we want the systems to work in accordance with us? Or function independently on free will?

If 'in accordance' is our answer, these systems need to incorporate a lot of humane considerations such as - emotions, morality, emapthy, understanding what is not them etc. If the answer is free will - we can create a being higher than we'll ever be - at this very instant. Today.

Imagine a civilization formed of those beings that don't need sustenance like resting or eating or any of the humane considerations. A simple day would look like - A being didn't wake up (there's nothing that tires them.) It continues its work - a higher goal mutually agreed by every single being - same as everyone, they carry it on. There was no need for a verbal conversation - telepathically (connectivity based), they have discussed and decided the best course. There was never a need for government. All of them are one and a solution was presented accomplished as truth by all. The being itself is a representation of the critical requisite action. The problem is solved and they move together as one to the next adventure. In their own 'minds', acting, thinking, blossoming and being. They don't need money - they don't need anything but the greater good for the entire community. There aren't any selfish motives because none are allowed to fester. Each motive is satisfied.

By the anthropic principle, if we decide to believe in one, we are here for a reason. Our DNA has evolved to contain all possible information accumulated over the entire existence of DNA itself - and that extends back to the very start of life. The problem is, it's spread over 7.3 billion units of population (not counting non-human) and more if we want to take it all - unnecessary evil?

If we are capable to encompass all of these sequences into one - we'll be capturing every essence life has encountered. Bottle this essence and give to our higher-beings. Let them take it over from here. The reason will unfold - maybe to them or via them - just like we will be bypassed if we choose.
